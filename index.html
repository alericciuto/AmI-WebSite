Project Vision


Testo da inserire descrittivo. Per test si è usata la descrizione del doc finale.

Every year a huge percentage of road accidents are caused by people who fall asleep while driving.
This AmI System wants to avoid these unpleasant situations by monitoring driver’s physiological parameters (such as heartbeat and the pressure applied on the steering wheel) and his attention span.
Thanks to the verification of these parameters and to the analysis of the answers given to the questions proposed by the system, it is able to react in order to keep the user active through sound stimulation, seat and steering wheel vibration.
Moreover, Keep Me Awake interacts with the driver when it detects that he’s about to fall asleep suggesting him to stop driving and rest or keeping him active through surveys and conversations of his interest.
